Waste Management

=======

What is waste?

Waste is a subtance that is discarded after its first use. The waste that we mostly produce in NYC is known as Municipal Solid Waste which mainly consits of household trash.

Basics on Recycling

Common items that you can recycle in everyday life are soda cans, newspapers, water bottles and more.
You can drop off recycables at a collection center near you or wait for curbside collection in your community.
It doesn't count as recycling if you just mix up all regular trash with things that are made of plastic, metal, or paper.

Why should you recycle?

Recycling has many benefits to both the economy and the environment.
